Age of Apocolypse

Information

  • Writers: Brian Michael Bendis
  • Plot Points: In a twisted version of the world they knew, the X-Men battle against the eternal mutant Apocalypse as Bishop seeks to repair the timeline. Legion, Xavier's own son, attempts to kill off all of Xavier's enemies; however, when Legion attempts to murder Magneto, Xavier sacrifices his own life to save Magnus. As a result, Magneto casts off his anti-human sentiments and carries on Xavier's dream of peaceful co-existence, thereby founding the X-Men.
  • Characters: Mystique, Wild Child, Abyss, Quicksilver
  • Date: March, 1995 - June, 1996
  • Illustrators: Joe Madureira, Roger Cruz, Steve Epting
